Routine

  • Morning Routine

    1. Scoop Snowy litter

    2. Fill kibbles

    3. Fill water bowl and water fountain if empty

    4. Give wet food if needed.

  • Evening Routine

    1. Scoop Snowy litter

    2. Fill kibbles if empty

    3. Fill water fountain

    4. Wash snowy water bowl and fill fresh water

Instructions

  • Snowy Litter
    • 1. Scoop 2x a day to prevent smell
    • 2. Change litter and wash tray every 2 weeks / when smelly
    • 3. Add baking powder first before adding litter
  • Dry Kibbles
    • 1. Fill up food every day
    • 2. Throw kibble and replace with new one if soggy
    • 3. Wash bowls every 2 weeks
  • Water Fountain/Bowl
    • 1. Fill up every day
    • 2. Change water filter and clean fountain every 2 weeks
    • 3. Vacuum fur in surrounding area
    • 4, Fill up Snowy water bowl every day
    • 5. Wash Snowy water bowl to get rid of fur every few days
  • Grooming
    • 1. Brush Shuki fur every day
    • 2. Or send to All Bout cats: 9785 6774
    • 3. Wipe cats with baby wipes
